Healthy-ish Peanut Butter Cookies!


Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1/4 Cup of Honey
  • 3/4 Cup of Crunchy Peanut Butter
  • 2 tbsp Coconut Sugar (or 2 more tbsp of honey)
  • 3/4 Cup Almond Flour
  • 1/4 tsp Sea Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp Baking Soda

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees Farenheit
  2. Whisk together egg, vanilla extract and honey. Add in peanut butter and mix thoroughly.
  3. Fold in dry ingredients and mix thoroughly.
  4. Using a cookie scoop or two spoons, divide dough into a dozen cookies on parchment paper covered baking sheet.
  5. Using a wet fork, press gently down on dough, and once more at a 90-degree angle to make criss cross marks. Rinse fork for each cookie to prevent it from sticking to the dough.
  6. Bake for 14 minutes
  7. Remove from oven, add additional sea salt to personal flavor, and let cool.

The original recipe comes from Rachel DeVaux's Soft & Chewy Peanut Butter Cookies healthified
